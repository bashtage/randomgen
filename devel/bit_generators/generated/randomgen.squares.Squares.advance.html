    Squares.advance(delta)¶

    Advance the state of the PRNG

    Parameters:¶
    delta¶

    The number of steps to advance the PRNG. Must be an integer in the range [0, 2**64).

    Returns:¶

    self – The PRNG advanced by delta steps.
